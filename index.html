Speech/Text to Handwritten Text conversion

  • You can import your handwritten .ttf or .otf font file with "Upload your Handwriting Font" button.
  • Click in the speech recognition dialog box to enter your speech and the text will be generated.
  • Copy the text generated through speech by clicking on the copy button and paste it (ctrl + V) in the ruled sheet where the text will be converted into handwritten text.
  • Place the cursor on the sheet accordingly wherever adding or editing text.
  • Text can be entered manually into the sheet through typing into the keyboard too
  • You can customize and edit the text and the sheet of paper according to your needs from the customizations options given below.

Guide to add your own handwriting

  • To use your handwriting, you will have to generate font of your handwriting.
  • There are websites like Calligraphr that let you do that.
  • Once you get .ttf file of your handwriting, upload it from 'Upload your handwriting font' button in customizations sections and select your .ttf file
